# 📊 Task 1: Business Sales Performance Dashboard

## 🔍 Objective
To analyze business sales data and extract meaningful insights regarding sales trends, product performance, and regional distribution.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel

---

## 📊 Analysis Performed
- Sales trend over time
- Category-wise performance analysis
- Top-selling products identification
- Region-wise sales comparison

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Sales vary significantly across different regions
- A small number of products contribute to major revenue
- Certain categories consistently outperform others
- Sales trends show fluctuations over time

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Focus on high-performing regions to maximize revenue
- Promote top-selling products for better growth
- Improve performance of low-performing categories
- Optimize strategies based on sales trends
